CardQuest

Rules & Gameplay

  • A standard 52-card deck is shuffled, and four cards are drawn. In each round, a hidden card is revealed, and you must predict specific attributes of the card.
  • Correct answers award 2 points. Incorrect answers award 0 points. The total score is displayed after each round.
  • After the fourth card, players can choose to restart the game and play again with a reshuffled deck.
  • After 10 rounds, your final score is tallied. Keep pushing to beat your high score each time you play!
