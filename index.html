CardQuest

Rules & Gameplay

  • A standard 52-card deck is shuffled, and four cards are drawn. In each round, a hidden card is revealed, and you must predict specific attributes of the card
  • The rankings start with 2 as the lowest value and go up to Ace (A) as the highest
  • If the value of the second card matches the first, selecting 'higher' will earn you points. If the value of the third card matches either the first or second, choosing 'inside' will award you points
  • Correct answers earn you 2 points, while incorrect answers score 0 points. Your total score is displayed after each round and continues to accumulate after every restart
  • Challenge yourself to see how high you can score and how many correct guesses you can string together. Keep pushing your limits to beat your high score every time you play

      • Rounds

    • First Card - Color Guess: Predict the color of the first card (Red or Black)
    • Second Card - Higher or Lower: Gauge whether the second card’s value is higher or lower than the first card
    • Third Card - Between or Outside: Determine if the third card’s value is between or outside the first two cards' values
    • Fourth Card - Suit Guess: Predict the suit of the fourth card (Clubs, Diamonds, Hearts, Spades)
